---
name: "beads-task-shaping"
description: "Convert analysis output into actionable Beads-ready work items with clear scope, priority, and acceptance checks."
---

Turn findings or plans into Beads-ready tasks.

For each task:
- write a concrete title
- describe the problem or action clearly
- record why it matters
- give a priority based on delivery or risk impact
- add acceptance or verification notes

Do not create Beads items for trivial observations that do not merit tracking.
